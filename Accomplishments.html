Accomplishments Timeline

Here is a timeline of my greatest achievements!



Hover over the images for more information!

2009

In 2009, I built my first advanced LEGO set, the Star Wars Millennium Falcon, which was an exciting experience. I carefully assembled the intricate pieces and created the spaceship I had always wanted.

2013

My dad and I spent hours carefully constructing the Lego Death Star, following the instructions step by step. We had fun bonding over the project and admiring our completed masterpiece once it was done.

2016

I have always loved the ship design of the Slave 1, so I purchased the set and built it. It was a fun experience, and I was proud of the finished product.

2018

I loved the massive tank-like things in Star Wars The Empire Strikes Back as a child. It was a nostalgic trip back to my childhood, and the finished product was a source of great pride and joy.

2019

After building the AT-AT, I wanted to accompany it with an AT-ST, so I bought the set and built it. I love how the two sets look together.

2023

After Nintendo closed the E-Shop, I decided to hack my 3DS XL so I could play all the games I have yet to purchase. I can also play the games that I either lost or had been stolen from me!
