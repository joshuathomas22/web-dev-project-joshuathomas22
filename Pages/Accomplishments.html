2009

In 2009, I built my first advanced LEGO set, the Star Wars Millennium Falcon, which was an exciting experience. I carefully assembled the intricate pieces and created the spaceship I had always wanted.

2013

My dad and I spent hours carefully constructing the Lego Death Star, following the instructions step by step. We had fun bonding over the project and admiring our completed masterpiece once it was done.

2019

I wrote a short story while I was 14, and to my surprise, it was published in a book! In fact, I had no idea it happened until I was 15 and receiving an award for another short story! You can find my work on page 138.

2019

On page 80 of this book, you'll find a short story I wrote back when I was 15. I never thought this piece of work would be considered, let alone published, but here we are!

2020

After years of burning my fingers on my laptop, I bit the bullet and bought all the components for a computer. Despite much trial and error, I managed to build my first computer, and have since built several computers, as I really enjoy it!

2023
